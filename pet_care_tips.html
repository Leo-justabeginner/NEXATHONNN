Essential Pet Safety Tips

  • 🍫 Keep harmful foods like chocolate, grapes, and onions away from pets.
  • 🏡 Ensure they have a safe and comfortable place to rest.
  • 💧 Provide clean drinking water at all times.
  • ⚠️ Keep toxic household items out of reach.
  • 🩺 Regular vet check-ups are essential for health.
  • 🐕 Use a leash while walking pets in public areas.
  • 🔖 Ensure proper identification with collars or microchips.
